E Cell organized following activities since Jan 2019 for its student members of Patna collage , Nit patna &Patna university :

Workshop on " start up India : What's in it for budding entrepreneurs

E Cell 'NIT PATNA' organizes Workshop on 'start up India : What's in it for budding entrepreneurs' for student entrepreneurs on Saturday ,sep, 2019. Students were informed about start up India and what they can avail out of various govt initiatives.

E- session 'Motivation and Opportunities'

E Cell NIT PATNA organized E- Session on '' Motivation and Opportunities' for budding student entrepreneurs on Saturday 16 th Jan 2019 .During the session following activities were undertaken: Session on Motivation for entrepreneurship;Discussion on growth of startups started by students recently; New opportunities in the market.

Workshop : how to develop business plan for entrepreneurial finance

E Cell 'NIT PATNA' organized Workshop on ' How to develop Business plan for Entrepreneurial Finance' for budding student entrepreneurs from Saturday ,20 th Feb to 27 Feb, 2019 . The workshop focused on development of Business Plan in which a sample plan was provided to students followed by discussion on key inputs to the plan .

Orientation Program & Workshop 'Motivation for Entrepreneurship'

E Cell organized Orientation programme and workshop on 'Motivation for Entrepreneurship' for its members on Saturday, 13th Aug, 2019 .During the orientation session E Cell members were informed about the plan of action, entrepreneurs at MERI and discussions were held on their aspirations . Following the orientation , the workshop laid emphasis on motivating the members through discussion on market scenario , new entrepreneurial push by Govt, support structures and cases of startups.


The Context

The region of Patna is under developed terms of industrialization, there are not many good paying jobs available for the fresher management graduates. There are many students who want to start their own business in their cities and villages. The establishment of cell facilitates them to learn the art of doing business. There are many students who had traditional family business and the cell help them rejuvenate their business into modern business. The programs are designed in such a way that students get exposure in various fields and different business functions.
