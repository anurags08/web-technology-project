Goal

The purpose of the cell is to develop individuals across socio economic backgrounds, who have the courage to dream, the drive to make a difference, the ability to believe in themselves and the persistence to convert their thoughts into action. The cell works to prepare a pool of next generation entrepreneurs who, through their dreams and action.

Objective of the cell

Develop the capabilities of self employment among the students. Use their knowledge and skills fullest for their own venture. Establish their identity and can able to generate jobs for others. Rejuvenate the traditional family business in to modern business.

The Context

The region of Patna is under developed terms of industrialization, there are not many good paying jobs available for the fresher management graduates. There are many students who want to start their own business in their cities and villages. The establishment of cell facilitates them to learn the art of doing business. There are many students who had traditional family business and the cell help them rejuvenate their business into modern business. The programs are designed in such a way that students get exposure in various fields and different business functions.
